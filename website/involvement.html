Involvement

ENED Teaching Assistant

For my final year of school, I have been working as a teaching assistant for University of Cincinnati's first-year engineering courses, Engineering Design Thinking 1 and 2. These classes are designed to teach students engineering design concepts and also various programming languages. I have helped students learn programming basics like conditional logic flow, iterating loops, and functions. Languages covered in this class include Excel, LabView, Matlab, and Python.

I have enjoyed my experience with this class so far. I've found that I like working with students much more than I anticipated. I think helping students with programming is an enjoyable challenge. It requires me to think about how to best explain to beginners concepts I now take for granted. I also interact with students in a peer mentoring capacity. In this role, I have helped students become familiar with UC and prepare for their careers.

Bearcast Radio DJ

For a couple semesters, I had a radio show on UC's student radio service, Bearcast Media. I love music and had a great time with this project. Each week, I tried to balance an hour of new music and a themed hour of music. Some examples of themes I did were all covers, all artists from Canada, and all songs recorded or produced by one of my favorite artists, Brian Eno. This was a really cool experience and a nice break from thinking about my usual course work.
